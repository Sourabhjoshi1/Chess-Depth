About ChessDepth

Welcome to ChessDepth, an online chess academy built for passionate players who want to go deeper. Founded by FIDE-rated player Sourabh Joshi (1603 FIDE, 2100+ online), our goal is to help developing players break plateaus, understand the "why" behind every move, and grow into confident tournament players.

Whether you're just learning to control the center or preparing for national-level events, ChessDepth tailors lessons for every level under 1800. We don’t just throw tactics — we show you patterns, plans, and positional subtleties that actually win games.

At ChessDepth, we believe in personalized learning, practical feedback, and most importantly, enjoying the beautiful game of chess together.

Let's go beyond surface-level chess — let's go deep.
